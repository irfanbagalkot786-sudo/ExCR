System Overview

The **Classroom AI Monitor** is a sophisticated monitoring framework designed to provide educators with non-intrusive, real-time analytics on student engagement. By leveraging edge-based computer vision, the system identifies behavioral markers associated with attention, fatigue, and distraction.

  • Goal: Optimize classroom delivery through data-driven feedback.
  • Privacy: Localized processing ensures transient data handling.

Computer Vision Pipeline

Our vision pipeline utilizes the **MediaPipe Face Landmarker** solution, which provides high-fidelity face tracking even in low-light classroom environments.

Landmarking

478 3D landmarks are extracted per frame to monitor eye aspect ratios (EAR) and head pose orientation.

Blendshapes

Analyzing 52 blendshape categories to detect nuanced facial expressions like squinting or yawning.

Engagement Scoring Algorithm

The engagement score is a weighted composite of several behavioral vectors:

Vector Metric Weight
Gaze Consistency Pupil Intersection 40%
Posture Alertness Z-axis Stabilizer 30%
Ocular Refresh Blink Frequency 30%
