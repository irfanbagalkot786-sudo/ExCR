⚖️ Privacy & Ethics

Compliance frameworks and commitments to ethical AI facial analysis.

Privacy Protocol

Our system is designed with a **Privacy-First** architecture. Student biometric data is transiently processed and never stored in its raw visual form.

  • 🔒
    Transient Processing: Data streams are analyzed in real-time. Once frame analysis is complete, the pixel data is discarded from the buffer.
  • 👤
    Anonymization: Unique identifiers are assigned based on session IDs rather than name-based profiles.

Compliance Standards

FERPA Compliant

The software adheres to the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act by ensuring student records are only stored in aggregate engagement formats.

GDPR Ready

Users have the "Right to be Forgotten," with easily accessible session deletion controls for any processed data.

Ethics in Generative AI Monitoring

"AI should empower educators, not replace their intuition. Monitoring is and should remain a feedback loop for improvement, not individual surveillance."

Commitments:
  • Bias Reduction: Ensuring facial recognition robustness across diverse demographics.
  • Transparency: Clear indicators when the monitoring system is actively analyzing.
  • Consent-Driven: Monitoring is initiated only after explicit educator/admin confirmation.
